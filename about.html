Our Story

Musa Bookstore began as a dream in the heart of book lovers who believed that literature should be accessible, exciting, and ever-present in our communities. What started as an ambitious pop-up bookstore culture has evolved into a beloved destination for readers of all ages and preferences.

From humble beginnings setting up in different neighborhoods, market squares, and community centers, we traveled across the city bringing curated collections of books directly to passionate readers. Each pop-up location told a story, connected with a unique community, and created memorable experiences around the love of reading.

Our Journey

2020: The Beginning

Musa Bookstore launched as an independent pop-up vendor, bringing hand-selected books to weekend markets and community events. Our mission was simple: make books accessible and foster a love of reading in every neighborhood.

2021-2023: Changing Locations

We embraced the nomadic lifestyle, rotating through multiple neighborhoods and communities. Each location became a hub for book lovers—from downtown markets to suburban festivals, university campuses to cultural centers. Our pop-up events became anticipated gatherings where readers discovered new titles and connected with fellow book enthusiasts.

2024: Going Digital & Beyond

Recognizing the demand our pop-up presence created, we established our online store to serve our growing community of readers. Musa Bookstore now combines the convenience of digital shopping with the curated, personal touch that made our pop-ups special. We continue to honor our roots by carrying the diverse, thoughtfully selected inventory that defines us.
